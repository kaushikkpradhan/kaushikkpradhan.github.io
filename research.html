Research Projects

  1. Valles Caldera Imaging (PhD Dissertation)
    Joint Bayesian inversion of ambient-noise surface-wave dispersion and H/V spectral ratios to resolve shallow crustal structure and velocity discontinuities beneath a quaternary caldera system.
  2. EGS Collab – Fracture Characterization (PNNL)
    Coda-wave decorrelation and scattering cross-section density analysis of active seismic data to track hydraulic fracture development during enhanced geothermal system stimulation (SURF, South Dakota).
  3. Cape Modern Geothermal Field (LBNL)
    Waveform envelope modeling using radiative-transfer theory to separate intrinsic and scattering attenuation and infer subsurface fracture heterogeneity.
  4. Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS)
    Development of Python workflows for DAS data processing and surface-wave analysis in cryospheric and near-surface environments.
  5. Lg Attenuation – Western Himalaya
    Broadband waveform analysis to estimate Lg attenuation parameters (Q₀, η) and map lateral variations in crustal properties.
