Kaushik Pradhan

I am a PhD candidate in Geophysics at the University of Texas at El Paso, in the final year of my doctoral program. I expect to graduate in Summer 2026 and am seeking a postdoctoral position starting August 2026.

My research focuses on seismic imaging and inversion of volcanic and geothermal systems, with an emphasis on near-surface structure, fracture characterization, and attenuation.

My dissertation centers on imaging the Valles Caldera (New Mexico) using joint Bayesian inversion of ambient-noise surface-wave dispersion and H/V spectral ratios to resolve shallow crustal structure.
